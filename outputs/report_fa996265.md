## Recommended Mitigations

1. Establish and document a comprehensive Risk Management System (RMS) covering the entire lifecycle of the AI system as per Article 9.
2. Develop a Data Governance Policy and conduct a Quality Assessment Report to meet the data governance requirements outlined in Article 10.
3. Prepare and maintain comprehensive Technical Documentation in accordance with Annex IV before placing the system on the market, as required by Article 11.
4. Implement human-in-the-loop operational guidelines to ensure effective human oversight, as mandated by Article 14.
5. Conduct regular audits and assessments to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations regarding the use of biometric data.
