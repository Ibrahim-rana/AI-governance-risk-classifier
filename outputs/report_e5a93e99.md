## Recommended Mitigations

1. Implement a robust risk management system to ensure compliance with Article 9 of the EU AI Act.
2. Establish clear human oversight mechanisms to review automated decisions and provide an appeal process for applicants.
3. Ensure transparency in the decision-making process by documenting the algorithms and data used for training the AI models.
4. Conduct regular audits to assess the fairness and bias of the AI system, particularly regarding its impact on protected characteristics.
5. Enhance data governance practices to ensure the quality and provenance of the data used in the AI system.
