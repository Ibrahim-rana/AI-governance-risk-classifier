## Use Case Summary

**AI System:** AI-Based Medical Diagnosis Support System
**Description:** This AI system assists doctors in diagnosing diseases by analyzing medical data such as patient records, lab results, and medical images (e.g., X-rays, MRIs). The system provides diagnostic suggestions and risk scores, helping doctors make more accurate and faster decisions. It does not fully replace doctors but supports clinical decision-making.

## Recommended Mitigations

1. Implement robust data governance practices to ensure the quality and provenance of training data.
2. Establish clear human oversight mechanisms to review and validate AI-generated diagnostic suggestions.
3. Conduct regular bias assessments to ensure equitable outcomes across diverse patient demographics.
4. Ensure transparency by providing users with clear information about how the AI system operates and its decision-making processes.
5. Develop a comprehensive data retention policy that complies with GDPR requirements.
