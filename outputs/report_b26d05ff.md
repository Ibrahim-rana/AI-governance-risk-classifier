## Recommended Mitigations

1. Implement a transparent algorithm that allows for explainability of decisions made by the AI system.
2. Ensure human oversight is integrated into the hiring process to review and validate AI-generated recommendations.
3. Conduct regular audits to assess and mitigate any biases in the AI system's decision-making.
4. Establish clear data retention and minimization practices to protect candidate personal data.
5. Provide candidates with information about how their data is used and the role of AI in the hiring process.
