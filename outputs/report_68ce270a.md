## Use Case Summary

**AI System:** AI-Powered Resume Screening and Candidate Ranking System
**Description:** This AI system automatically analyses job applications to assist HR teams in recruitment. It processes candidate resumes using natural language processing (NLP) to extract key information such as education, skills, experience, and certifications. The system compares this data against job requirements and ranks candidates based on relevance and suitability.

The AI also identifies potential matches, flags missing qualifications, and may provide shortlisting recommendations. It can learn from past hiring decisions to improve future ranking accuracy. The system is used as a decision-support tool and does not make final hiring decisions without human review.
**Domain:** Employment
**Personal Data:** Yes

## Recommended Mitigations

1. Establish and document a comprehensive Risk Management System (RMS) covering the entire lifecycle of the AI system as per Article 9.
2. Develop a Data Governance Policy and conduct a Quality Assessment Report to ensure compliance with Article 10.
3. Prepare and maintain comprehensive Technical Documentation in accordance with Article 11 before the system is placed on the market.
4. Implement human oversight measures, including Human-in-the-loop operational guidelines and UI/UX evidence, to comply with Article 14.
5. Conduct regular audits to assess and mitigate biases in the AI system's decision-making processes.
