## Recommended Mitigations

1. Implement robust human oversight mechanisms to review AI decisions in the recruitment process.
2. Ensure transparency in the AI decision-making process by providing clear documentation on how the AI evaluates candidates.
3. Conduct regular audits to assess and mitigate any biases in the AI model that may affect protected characteristics.
4. Establish clear data retention policies and practices for personal data processed by the AI system.
5. Provide candidates with the ability to appeal or contest decisions made by the AI system.
