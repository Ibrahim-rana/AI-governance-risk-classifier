## Recommended Mitigations

1. Implement robust data protection measures to ensure compliance with GDPR regarding personal data processing.
2. Establish clear human oversight mechanisms to review AI decisions in the recruitment process.
3. Conduct regular audits to assess and mitigate potential biases in the AI screening algorithm.
4. Provide transparency to candidates about the use of AI in the recruitment process and how their data is being used.
5. Ensure that the training data used for the AI system is diverse and representative to minimize discrimination risks.
