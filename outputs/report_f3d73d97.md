## Use Case Summary

**AI System:** AI-Powered Resume Screening and Candidate Ranking System
**Description:** This AI system is designed to assist recruiters in screening job applications by automatically analyzing resumes and ranking candidates based on job requirements.

The system uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to extract key information such as skills, education, experience, and keywords from resumes. It compares this data with job descriptions and assigns a relevance score to each candidate.

A machine learning model, trained on historical hiring data, predicts candidate suitability and ranks applicants accordingly. The system provides recruiters with a shortlist of top candidates to improve hiring efficiency.

The tool is intended to reduce manual workload, speed up recruitment, and support data-driven hiring decisions. Human recruiters can review and override AI recommendations.
**Domain:** Human Resources (HR) / Recruitment
**Users Affected:** Job Applicants, Recruiters, HR Professionals

## Recommended Mitigations

1. Implement robust data protection measures to ensure compliance with GDPR regarding personal data processing.
2. Conduct regular audits to assess and mitigate potential biases in the AI model to ensure fairness in candidate evaluation.
3. Ensure transparency by providing clear documentation on how the AI system makes decisions and the criteria used for ranking candidates.
4. Establish a human-in-the-loop process to allow recruiters to review and override AI recommendations effectively.
5. Provide candidates with the ability to appeal decisions made by the AI system to enhance accountability.
