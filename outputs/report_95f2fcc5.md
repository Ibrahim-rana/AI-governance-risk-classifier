## Recommended Mitigations

1. Establish and document a comprehensive Risk Management System (RMS) covering the entire lifecycle of the AI system as per Article 9.
2. Develop and implement a Data Governance Policy and conduct a Quality Assessment Report to comply with Article 10.
3. Prepare comprehensive Technical Documentation in accordance with Annex IV before placing the system on the market as required by Article 11.
4. Create and implement Human-in-the-loop operational guidelines to ensure human oversight in decision-making processes as stipulated in Article 14.
5. Conduct regular audits and assessments to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations regarding the processing of biometric data.
